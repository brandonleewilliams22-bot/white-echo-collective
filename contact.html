Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with White Echo Collective.

How long do projects typically take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Brand identity projects typically take 4-6 weeks, while comprehensive website development can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with businesses of all sizes?

Yes, we work with startups, small businesses, and established companies. Our approach is tailored to your specific needs, goals, and budget regardless of your company size.

What's included in the initial consultation?

Our free consultation includes a thorough discussion of your goals, current challenges, target audience, and vision. We'll also provide initial insights and recommendations for your brand strategy.

How do you ensure projects stay on budget?

We provide detailed proposals with clear scope and pricing upfront. Any changes or additions are discussed and approved before implementation to ensure transparency and budget control.
